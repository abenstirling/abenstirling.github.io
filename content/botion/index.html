Support

For any urgent bugs or issues, please email help@botion.app.

Q1: How do I sign up for the app?

To sign up, download the app from the App Store, open it, and follow the on-screen instructions. You can sign up using your Notion account via secure OAuth.

Q2: What features does the app offer?

Our app offers:

  • Customizable task lists for daily planning
  • Integration with calendars for event tracking

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1/14/25

Information We Don't Collect

We do not collect any information from you when you use our app. There is no registration required, and we don't gather data through the app's features. Therefore, you can use our app anonymously.

Data Usage

Since we don't collect any data, there is no data usage to discuss. Our app is designed to respect your privacy fully.

Security Measures

Although we don't collect information, we still prioritize the security of our app to protect any interactions you may have while using it.

Data Retention Policy

There is no data retention policy to discuss, as we do not collect or store any data.

Cookies and Similar Technologies

Our app does not use cookies or similar tracking technologies.

Third-Party Involvement

We do not share any information with third parties, primarily because we don't collect any. There are no third-party products or services integrated into our app that would require us to share data.

Children's Online Privacy Protection Act Compliance

Our app is directed to people who are at least 13 years old or older. However, since we do not collect data, there are no COPPA compliance requirements.

Applicability

This privacy policy applies only to the use of this app and not to any other interactions you may have with us.

Your Consent

By using our app, you acknowledge that you have read and understand this privacy policy.

Changes to Our Privacy Policy

If there are any changes to this privacy policy, they will be updated here.
