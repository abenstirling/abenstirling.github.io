Botion Heavyweight

1lb Plastic Antweight Combat Robot

Combat Strategy

1. High Strength-to-Weight Ratio

Utilizing plastic construction to maximize the strength-to-weight ratio in the 1lb weight class. This approach prioritizes maneuverability and structural integrity over raw damage output.

2. Immobilization Focus

Primary strategy revolves around immobilizing opponent robots rather than causing destructive damage. This approach is more consistent and reliable in tournament settings.

3. Wedge Design

Front-mounted wedge design to get under opponents and control the fight. The wedge serves as both offensive and defensive elements.

4. Centered Weapon System

Single, centrally-mounted weapon integrated into the wedge design for maximum effectiveness while maintaining balance and control.

5. Reversible Drive System

Designed to be driven from either orientation, ensuring the robot can continue fighting effectively even if flipped.

6. TPU Entanglement Features

Rear-mounted TPU stringy elements designed to entangle and further immobilize opponent robots, adding another layer to the control-based strategy.
