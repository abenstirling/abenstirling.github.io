Typing passwords sucks. 

I have two issues: 
- I really like having my laptop docked 
- I like mechancial keyboards 

These two desires keep me gated from access to Apple's fingerprint to autofill passwords! Unfair.

Lets change that... 

## Inspo
This has been done lots over the internet, but I referenced [Jeff Geerling's guide](https://www.jeffgeerling.com/blog/2025/why-doesnt-apple-make-standalone-touch-id/) so yeah follow that for instructions, this is just a commentary of my experience building one!
